About us

Welcome to the Comox Valley Toy Library Society!

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet impactful, to provide a diverse range of clean, high-quality toys that spark joy and encourage learning among young children. We believe in making play accessible to all, offering an eco-friendly alternative that supports our community's enviromental goals. At CVTLS, we're more than just a library; we're a community-drive initiative dedicated to enriching the lives of young children and supporting families and caregivers. Operated by a passionate team of volunteers, we are a non-profit organization commited to offering a sustainable and bidget-friendy solution for families seeking quality toys.

¿Why Choose us?

We offer an enviromentally conscious approach to play bye lending toys, reducing waste and promoting the reuse of resources.

CVTLS is not just about toys: it's about building connections and fostering a supportive community for families.

Understanding the financial challenges many families face, we provide a cost-effective option to access a wide range of toys.

Join us in our journey to bring smiles to children's faces while making a positive impact on the planet. Explore our toy collection and become a part of the CVTLS family today!
