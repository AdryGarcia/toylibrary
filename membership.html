Become a member!

¿What is a Toy Library Membership?

A CVTLS membership is $20 a year, giving your family the ability to take out any 5 toys at a time to keep for a whole month. The Toy Library offers three openings a month, providing endless opportunities for play!

Why should I join?

Access to a large variety of quality toys

Borrow toys that match your child's developmental stage and interests.

Budget friendly. Membership are only $20 annually

Support the environment by lending and being sustainable.

Enjoy a clutter-free home with instant toy rotation.

The opportunity to try before you buy new toys.

Register

¿How to Join?

  • Come in to the Toy Library during open hours.
  • Provide one form of personal identification.
  • Read and sign the membership in person.
  • Pay the annual membership fee of $20.
  • Start borrowing toys!

Financial Assistance. The Comox Velley Toy Library (CVTLS) warmy welcomes all families and caregivers in the community, emphasizing inclusivity and support regardless of income level or financial hardship. We believe every child deserves the joy and learning opportunities that come from play. If the membership cost is a barrier, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
