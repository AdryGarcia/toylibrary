Our Mission

The CVTLS is a non-profit society operated by volunteers that offers a clean, affordable, and enviromentally conscious alternative by lending toys to families and caregivers of young children.

This Toy Library is ru by a fun group of volunteers made up of parents, grandparents, and commnunity members. We volunteer because we believe in modeling sharing, and we have a lot of fun doing it! We can always use more volunteers. Join us!

Another way to give back would be by donating some toys. We currently only accept donations of quality, washable toys with all of their pieces. We ask you to think about your donations like this: Is it still in excellent condition? Would other parents or caregivers choose this toy from the shelf over others? Would I borrow this toy myself? If yes, we would love the opportunity to share it with the children in our commnunity.

We lend sanitizable toys to our members for $20 per year. This cost covers our liability insurance. If cost is a barrier to joining, please contact us. We want this fantastic resource to be available for everyone.
