Volunteer

Do you have two hours a month to give?

Join our team!

Make an impact

Volunteering at the Comox Valley Toy Library is an easy and impactful way to support your community. You'll play a key role in keeping this valuable resource available to families.

Volunteer you way

No prior training is required to volunteer with CVTLS; we welcome all skillsets and abilities. Whatever your background or interests, there's a place for you.

Bring yor kids!

Spending time exploring and testing toys helps create a vibrant, family-friendly atmosphere. Volunteering with your children teaches the value of giving back.
